Our Mission

Earworm is an ear-training webapp designed to hand control over to the user. The user can practice virtual piano and train have pitch correctiong training. Users and also leave feedback about the webapp on comment pace.


About us

We are a team of two software developers currently studying for a Bachelor's degree.
Ethan first started coding in his sophomore year in high school with a couple of java classes and has been hooked ever since. While he had always been interested in music, he took a much greater interest in piano at the start of 2021. When he started searching for a way to improve his perfect pitch, he was disappointed by the limited number of options at his disposal.
Sajal has always been interested in music. He picked up guitar while he was young, however, he didn't have much idea about pitch and had a hard time learning about it. While brainstorming about project ideas for a class, Ethan came up with the idea of the web app that could let the user help in their music journey. Sajal was really interested by the idea as well and decided to work together on it with Ethan.
